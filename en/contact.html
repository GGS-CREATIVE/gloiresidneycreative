FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are your timelines?
It depends on the project: a photo session can be booked within a few days, while a showcase site usually takes 3 to 5 weeks. We set a realistic schedule together from the start.
Do you work remotely?
Yes. I'm based in Brussels but work with clients everywhere. Web and branding projects are handled remotely; for photo and video, we arrange an on-site shoot.
How does payment work?
Usually a deposit at kickoff and the balance on delivery. The exact terms are detailed in the quote I send after our chat.
Can you take over an existing project?
Absolutely. I can improve a site, an identity or a set of visuals already started, without going back to square one unless needed.
